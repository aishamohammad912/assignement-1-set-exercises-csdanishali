Plot

*Sleepy Hollow* follows Ichabod Crane, a soldier from the Revolutionary War who wakes up in modern-day Sleepy Hollow. He discovers that he is connected to an ancient battle between good and evil. Teaming up with police lieutenant Abbie Mills, the two face off against supernatural threats — including the legendary Headless Horseman — as they uncover secrets about their pasts and a destiny that binds them to the fate of the world.
