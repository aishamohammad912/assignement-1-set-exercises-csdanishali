Sleepy Hollow

*Sleepy Hollow* is a supernatural drama television series that aired from 2013 to 2017. It reimagines Washington Irving’s classic tale of Ichabod Crane and the Headless Horseman, blending history with modern-day horror, mystery, and fantasy elements.

Key Information

  • Genre: Supernatural, Mystery, Drama
  • Seasons: 4
  • Original Network: Fox
  • Air Dates: 2013 – 2017
Visit Official Website

Cast & Creative Team

Created by Alex Kurtzman, Roberto Orci, Phillip Iscove, and Len Wiseman. The main cast includes:

  • Tom Mison as Ichabod Crane
  • Nicole Beharie as Abbie Mills
  • Lyndie Greenwood as Jenny Mills
  • Katia Winter as Katrina Crane
  • Orlando Jones as Frank Irving

Main Characters

Ichabod Crane: A Revolutionary War soldier resurrected in modern-day Sleepy Hollow.

Abbie Mills: A strong-willed police lieutenant who becomes Ichabod’s partner in facing dark forces.

Jenny Mills: Abbie’s rebellious sister with a deep knowledge of the occult.

Reviews

Critics praised *Sleepy Hollow* for its unique premise and engaging chemistry between the leads. The first season received particular acclaim, though later seasons had mixed responses. Still, fans appreciate its bold storytelling and supernatural flair.
