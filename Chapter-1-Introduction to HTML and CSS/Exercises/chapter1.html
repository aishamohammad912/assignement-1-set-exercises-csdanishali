Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Chocolate Chip Cookies

This classic chocolate chip cookie recipe yields 24 delicious, chewy cookies. Perfect for sharing or keeping all to yourself!

Ingredients

  • 360 g All-purpose Flour
  • 5 g Baking Soda
  • 5 g Salt
  • 230 g Unsalted Butter (Softened)
  • 200 g Granulated Sugar
  • 200 g Light Brown Sugar
  • 10 g Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 350 g Chocolate Chips/Chunks

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 180Â°C.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine room-temperature butter, both sugars, and vanilla. Mix until smooth. Add eggs and beat in.
  3. In another bowl, mix flour, baking soda, and salt. Add gradually to butter mixture and mix well.
  4. Stir in the chocolate chips or chunks.
  5. Scoop dough onto a baking sheet using a cookie scoop or spoon, spacing them well apart.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven on the middle rack for 10 minutes or until edges are lightly browned.
  7. Let cool slightly and enjoy warm or cold!
